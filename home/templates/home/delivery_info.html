Delivery

Please read below our delivery policy

At Floral Design we offer a simple and cheap delivery solution for our customers. Please take a moment to read our Delivery policy below.

We endevour to ship out all orders within a day of ordering. This ensures that your products will arrive within 2 working days of ordering.

Delivery costs

If you spend £60 or more on one purchase, delivery will be absolutely free.

All orders below £60 will be subject to a small delivery fee.

This delivery fee, will automaticlaly be added to your final spend total and will be clwarly displaye on the checkout page.

As of November 2024, the cost of shipping is 2 % of the total order, on a spend of below £40. A spend above £40 will be charged 5% of the overall cost for delivery. For an order £60 and above delivery will be free.
